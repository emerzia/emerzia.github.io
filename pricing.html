Emerzia Metropolitan


High capacity, high speed Emerzia system deployed on multiple servers with AMD, Performance Tuning, SMS capability, Signup Page, Training, Ongoing Support.

High specification servers required, according to discussions with the Emerzia Deployment Team.

Suitable for high populations in Metropolitan areas, including states, urban areas including large towns and cities, or for ultra high speed calling, such as nuclear, chemical site localities and tsunami at risk areas as part of a mass evacuation plan.

*Costs dependant on the required speed and call capacity which could be in excess of 1 million calls per hour.

Unlimited support included
